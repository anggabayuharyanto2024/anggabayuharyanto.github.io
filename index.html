About Me

I am an indie game developer and programmer with a strong passion for learning new things and achieving great feats to enhance my experiences.

Working at Telkom Indonesia has greatly encouraged me to collaborate with many talented individuals who have great minds and innovative concepts. Over the years, I have honed my skills in Excel, Database Management, Python, Web Development, and Game Development as a way to further my self-development.

Beyond my professional work, I enjoy reading and gaming in my spare time. Occasionally, I travel to relaxing and calming places for peace of mind. These activities enrich my creativity and provide new insights for many fresh ideas.

I am always ready to collaborate and share ideas on various projects. Feel free to connect with me on social media below—hopefully, we can become good friends!
